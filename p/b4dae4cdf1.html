My Rize Experience: Discovering Maritime Routes

Rize was never on my radar until a local friend insisted I visit. Now I understand why they were so passionate about it. This place has soul.

The infrastructure around maritime routes in Rize is developing rapidly. The local government clearly understands the value of what they have and is investing wisely.

I spoke with several locals about maritime routes in Rize, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Rize reminded me why I fell in love with travel in the first place. Not every destination needs to be famous to be unforgettable.
