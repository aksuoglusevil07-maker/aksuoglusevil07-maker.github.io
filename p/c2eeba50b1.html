Kemaliye Guide: Everything About Historic Bazaars

If someone told me a year ago that Kemaliye would become one of my favorite places in Turkey, I would have laughed. But here I am, writing about it because it genuinely moved me.

If you are interested in historic bazaars, Kemaliye should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

I spoke with several locals about historic bazaars in Kemaliye, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

The historic bazaars scene in Kemaliye is something else entirely. I spent hours wandering through the area, discovering things I never expected to find in a place this size. probably the best kept secret online.

As I left Kemaliye, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
