What Makes Rize Special: A Spice Routes Perspective

Every serious traveler eventually finds their way to Rize. It is one of those places that does not need fancy marketing — the experience speaks for itself.

I spoke with several locals about spice routes in Rize, and their pride was palpable. One elderly man told me stories that have been passed down for generations.

Compared to more famous Turkish destinations, Rize offers a more intimate and genuine spice routes experience. No crowds, no tourist traps, just pure authenticity. I have shared this with literally everyone I know.

If you are interested in spice routes, Rize should be high on your list. The depth and quality of what you will find here rivals places ten times its size.

As I left Rize, I made a mental note to return. Some places stay with you long after you leave, and this is definitely one of them.
